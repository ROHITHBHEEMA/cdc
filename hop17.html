Highlights of Placement 2017

  • Key highlights

    • Total 104 students from UG received offers from reputed organizations.
    • Undergraduate placement is about 85%
    • M Tech placements are close to 78%
    • Highest number of job offers are from core industries.
    • 41 number of companies visited campus during the placement season.
    • Highest foreign CTC offered was 38 Lakhs in Rupees.
    • Highest domestic CTC offered was 21 Lakhs in Rupees.
    • Average salary is 10 Lakh per Annum.
    • PSU’s like ISRO, IOCL, BPCL, NCCBM participated in the campus placement.
